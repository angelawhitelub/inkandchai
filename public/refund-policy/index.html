Refund Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

We want you to love every book you receive. If something goes wrong, we'll make it right.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a full refund in the following cases:

  • You received a damaged or defective book
  • You received the wrong book (different title/edition than ordered)
  • Your order was not delivered within 15 business days of dispatch
  • Your order was cancelled before dispatch

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Books that have been read, damaged by the customer, or are missing pages due to customer handling
  • Orders where the incorrect delivery address was provided by the customer
  • Change of mind after the order has been dispatched
  • Digital products, if any

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, email us at orders@inkandchai.in within 7 days of receiving your order with:

  • Your order ID
  • A clear photo of the damaged/incorrect item
  • Your registered phone number or email

4. Refund Processing Time

  • Online payments (Razorpay/PhonePe): Refund credited within 5–7 business days to your original payment method
  • Cash on Delivery orders: Refund via bank transfer (NEFT/UPI) within 7–10 business days after confirmation. You will need to provide your bank details.

5. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch by contacting us at orders@inkandchai.in. Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible — please refer to our Return Policy instead.

For COD orders, cancellations must be requested within 12 hours of placing the order.

6. Contact

For refund queries: orders@inkandchai.in

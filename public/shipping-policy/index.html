Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026 · Ink & Chai, New Delhi – 110006, India · support@inkandchai.in

We ship pan-India via trusted courier partners. All orders are packed carefully to ensure your books arrive in perfect condition.

1. Shipping Coverage

We deliver to all serviceable pincodes across India. Use the pincode checker on our homepage to verify delivery availability for your area.

2. Delivery Timeframes

Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, public holidays, or unforeseen circumstances.

3. Order Processing

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of placement.
  • Orders placed after 5:00 PM IST or on Sundays/public holidays are processed the next business day.
  • You will receive a dispatch confirmation email/SMS with tracking details once your order is shipped.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your shipment on the courier partner's website. For tracking assistance, contact us at support@inkandchai.in.

5. Free Shipping

Free shipping is available on all orders above ₹499 to eligible metro and Tier-1 locations. This threshold may be subject to change during special sale events.

6. Failed Delivery

If a delivery attempt fails (incorrect address, recipient unavailable, access issues), the courier will attempt re-delivery. After 2–3 failed attempts, the package may be returned to us. In such cases, re-delivery charges may apply.

7. Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged due to transit handling, please photograph the package and item upon receipt and email us at support@inkandchai.in within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or refund as per our Refund Policy.

8. Contact

For shipping queries: support@inkandchai.in

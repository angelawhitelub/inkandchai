Return Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026 · Ink & Chai, New Delhi – 110006, India · support@inkandchai.in

We accept returns for books that are damaged, defective, or incorrectly delivered. Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for Return

A return request will be accepted only if:

  • The book is in the same condition as received (for wrong item returns)
  • The original packaging is intact, or photographic evidence of damage is provided
  • The return is initiated within 7 days of the delivery date
  • The order ID and proof of purchase are provided

Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Books damaged after delivery due to customer handling
  • Items returned without prior approval from our team
  • Orders placed more than 7 days ago
  • Books purchased during special clearance or final-sale events

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email support@inkandchai.in with your Order ID and photos of the item.
  2. Our team will review your request within 2 business days and confirm eligibility.
  3. If approved, we will arrange a reverse pickup from your address at no cost to you.
  4. Once the returned item is received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Exchange Option

Instead of a refund, you may request an exchange for the same title (in case of a damaged copy) or the correct title (in case of a wrong item). Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

Return Shipping

For approved returns, Ink & Chai will arrange reverse pickup at no charge to the customer. Do not ship items back without our prior approval, as we cannot process unapproved returns.

Contact

Response time: Within 2 business days
